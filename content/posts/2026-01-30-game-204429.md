Moon Active has just launched Jelly Busters: Puzzle Game, an engaging arcade-style puzzler that invites players into a vibrant skatepark filled with elastic jellies. This game stands out by combining the thrill of shooting color with the satisfaction of uncovering hidden images as players break through stacks of glowing cubes. The unique gameplay mechanics not only provide a soothing ASMR experience but also keep players on their toes with increasing challenges.

As you progress through Jelly Busters, the game introduces tougher arrangements and obstacles, making each level a delightful test of skill and strategy. The competitive aspect, with leaderboards tracking global performance, adds an extra layer of excitement, encouraging players to refine their techniques and aim for the top. With unlockable boosters and rewards, every session promises new surprises and achievements.

Jelly Busters: Puzzle Game is more than just a casual title; its a captivating blend of relaxation and challenge that appeals to puzzle enthusiasts. Will you join the jelly crew and conquer the colorful skatepark?
