Get ready for a wild ride as the highly anticipated urban survival game, "Good Luck," is set to debut on PC on April 8, 2026. Developed by the innovative Turkish studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer challenges players to navigate a chaotic city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, it promises to deliver an exhilarating experience where survival is anything but guaranteed.

"Good Luck" stands out with its unique blend of humor and high-stakes gameplay, where even the most mundane objects can become deadly obstacles. The absence of checkpoints adds to the thrill, pushing players to master the unpredictable physics and chaotic environment. As it gears up for its release on PC, followed by launches on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S, gamers are left wondering: will they have what it takes to survive the urban jungle?
