In a remarkable achievement, *Pokemon Legends: Z-A* has emerged as the best-selling physical game in the US for 2025, just weeks after its release. According to Mat Piscatella, senior director at Circana, this title has not only topped the charts but has also set a new benchmark for retail launches in the gaming industry, surpassing sales figures of major titles like *The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom*.

The game's success is a testament to the enduring popularity of the Pokemon franchise, which continues to dominate various categories, including toys and trading cards. With 5.8 million copies sold globally in its first week, *Pokemon Legends: Z-A* is proving to be a significant player in the gaming landscape, appealing to both long-time fans and newcomers alike. As we look ahead, it will be interesting to see how this title performs during the holiday season and if it can maintain its momentum against potential competitors.
