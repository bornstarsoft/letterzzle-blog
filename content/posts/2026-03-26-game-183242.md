Toukana Interactive has just announced an exciting new Medieval Biome Pack for their beloved puzzle game, Dorfromantik, set to launch in May/June 2026. This expansion promises to transform the games serene landscapes into a charming medieval setting, complete with historical makeovers for iconic tiles, such as a medieval trading cog and a mighty fortress. This cosmetic DLC is sure to captivate both new players and long-time fans alike, enhancing the cozy gameplay experience.

In addition to the Biome Pack, a free Anniversary Update will also be rolled out, introducing integrated leaderboards and a much-anticipated Night Mode. The Night Mode will provide a soothing visual experience, perfect for late-night gaming sessions. As Dorfromantik celebrates its 5th anniversary, these updates mark a significant milestone for the indie studio, showcasing their commitment to community feedback and continuous improvement. 

With these exciting developments on the horizon, fans are left wondering how these new features will further enrich the gameplay. Will you be diving back into the world of Dorfromantik to explore its medieval charm?
