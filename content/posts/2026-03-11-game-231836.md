This March, PlayStation Plus subscribers are in for a treat as six new titles have been leaked ahead of their official announcement. Among the most anticipated additions are Madden NFL 26 and Persona 5 Royal, both of which promise to enhance the gaming experience for fans of the platform. The leak comes from the reliable insider billbil-kun, known for accurately predicting upcoming game releases and hardware announcements.

In addition to the aforementioned titles, players can look forward to Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine 2, Metal Eden, The Lord of the Rings: Return to Moria, and Astroneer. Furthermore, the Classics Catalog will welcome the PSP classic Tekken Dark Resurrection, adding a nostalgic touch for long-time fans. All these games are expected to be available starting March 17, with an official announcement from Sony anticipated on March 11.
