Get ready for an emotional ride with "Save My Scrap," a visual novel and puzzle game that dives deep into the themes of connection and memory through the lens of android repair. Set to launch on Steam on April 22, 2026, this game is not just about fixing mechanical parts; its about mending the bonds between characters and uncovering their pasts. With a rich narrative and stunning cyberpunk visuals, players will find themselves immersed in a world where every repair reveals a story.

Developed by Unio Anou and published by AMATA Games, "Save My Scrap" builds on the success of its predecessor, "Fix My Junk," offering enhanced gameplay features, including new dialogues, endings, and a gallery mode. The games ability to evoke emotional responses while challenging players with puzzles makes it a standout title in the indie game scene.
