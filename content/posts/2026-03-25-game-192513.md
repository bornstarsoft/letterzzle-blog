In a world where productivity and optimization reign supreme, its refreshing to hear about the late-night guilty pleasures of public figures. Zac Clark, the beloved winner of Season 16 of 'The Bachelorette,' recently shared that his go-to way to unwind is playing a free block puzzle game called Blocky Blast. This candid admission not only humanizes him but also highlights the importance of finding moments of joy and relaxation amidst our busy lives.

Clark, who is also the CEO of Release Recovery, balances a demanding schedule filled with marathon training, meetings, and advocacy for addiction recovery. Yet, like many of us, he sometimes finds himself glued to his phone, indulging in a game instead of adhering to his structured evening routine. This small detail serves as a reminder that even the most disciplined individuals have their moments of indulgence, making them relatable to a global audience.

As we navigate our own hectic lives, perhaps we can take a page from Clarks book and embrace our guilty pleasures without guilt. Whats your favorite way to unwind after a long day? Let's celebrate those moments of authenticity that remind us were all in this together.
