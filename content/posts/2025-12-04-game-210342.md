The recent update 1.1.0 for Pokmon Friends has introduced a plethora of new features that are sure to enhance the gaming experience for players on Android, iOS, and Nintendo Switch. With the addition of new puzzles, plushies, and furniture, the game not only expands its content but also enriches the gameplay with new friend quests and enhanced customization options.

Among the most notable changes is the increase in the maximum number of plushies players can place from 20 to 25, allowing for even more creative expression in their virtual spaces. Additionally, the introduction of difficulty levels for untangling puzzles provides a tailored experience, making it accessible for both new and seasoned players. This update reflects The Pokmon Company's commitment to keeping the game fresh and engaging.
