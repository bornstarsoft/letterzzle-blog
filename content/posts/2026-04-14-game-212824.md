The upcoming release of 'Walk The Frog' on May 21st, 2026, promises to be a delightful experience for gamers seeking a cozy narrative puzzle adventure. Developed by Walk The Frog Studio, this game invites players to explore a whimsical world through the eyes of Froggo, engaging with sticky note puzzles that encourage creativity and thoughtful exploration. 

With hand-drawn visuals and a calming audio landscape, 'Walk The Frog' is designed to resonate with players of all ages, making it a perfect choice for families and casual gamers alike. The heartfelt story revolves around small discoveries and the connections we forge, reminding us of the beauty in life's simple moments.

As the developers, Oliver and Fabian, reflect on their journey since 2020, they express their excitement to finally share Froggo's world with a broader audience. With a growing community and a heartfelt narrative, 'Walk The Frog' is set to capture the hearts of many. Will you be joining Froggo on this charming adventure?
