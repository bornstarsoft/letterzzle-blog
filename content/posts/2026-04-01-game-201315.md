Dinosaur Polo Club has returned to their game jam roots with the release of RTFM, a unique co-op puzzle game that invites players to navigate the chaotic environment of a fictional corporation. Released on April Fools Day, this experimental title encourages collaboration and communication between players as they tackle surreal puzzles while avoiding the lurking threat of a ghost in the machine. The games design is a testament to the creativity that emerges during the studio's Creativity Week, showcasing how game jams can lead to innovative and engaging experiences.

In RTFM, players take on the roles of Troubleshooter and Operator, working together to decode puzzles and maintain their grip on reality. The game cleverly blends humor with tension, drawing inspiration from beloved titles and sci-fi media. With its brief yet replayable format, RTFM not only provides entertainment but also serves as a reminder of the importance of teamwork and trust in both gaming and real-life scenarios.

As we look ahead, its exciting to see how projects like RTFM can inspire other developers to embrace creativity and experimentation. Will we see more bite-sized games born from the passion of game jams?
