In an exciting announcement for gamers and horror enthusiasts alike, Game Pop Studio has revealed their upcoming title, *They Will Come*, a steampunk horror adventure puzzle game set to launch on PC via Steam in 2026. This game promises to immerse players in a richly detailed alternate history where they will navigate a perilous airship, battling sinister foes known as the Wretched Ones alongside their giant robot companion, Talus.

Players will step into the shoes of Benjamin, facing challenges that require stealth, puzzle-solving, and item collection to survive the dangers lurking aboard the Endeavor. With its captivating steampunk visuals and a bittersweet narrative, *They Will Come* aims to deliver a unique gaming experience that blends horror with intricate storytelling. As we await its release, it raises the question: how will this game redefine the horror genre within the steampunk aesthetic?
