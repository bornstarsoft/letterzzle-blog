November is shaping up to be an exciting month for Xbox Game Pass subscribers, with the announcement of 11 new titles set to join the service. Among these, the highly anticipated **Call of Duty: Black Ops 7** will be available for Game Pass Ultimate and PC Game Pass users starting November 14. This addition is sure to thrill fans of the franchise and those looking to dive into the latest action-packed gameplay.

The lineup includes a diverse range of games, from indie gems like **Dead Static Drive** and **Whiskerwood** to established favorites such as **Lara Croft and the Temple of Osiris**. With titles available across cloud, console, and PC, players have plenty of options to choose from, ensuring that theres something for everyone this month. However, its worth noting that six games will be leaving the service on November 15, so players should act quickly to enjoy those before theyre gone.

As Xbox continues to expand its Game Pass offerings, it raises the question: what other titles do you hope to see added in the future? With such a strong lineup this November, the possibilities are endless!
