The excitement is building as BeXide announces that their popular multiplayer party game, Fruit Mountain Party, will be making its way to the Switch 2 and the original Switch in Spring 2026. Currently available in Early Access on PC, this vibrant 3D puzzle game has captured the hearts of players with its unique fruit-combining mechanics and engaging multiplayer modes.

Fruit Mountain Party offers a delightful mix of competitive gameplay, featuring both online and local multiplayer options. Players can engage in heated battles with friends or take on the world in a single-player mode that supports global rankings. With its easy-to-learn mechanics and the thrill of stacking fruits to create massive combos, the game promises to be a hit among casual gamers and competitive players alike.

As we look forward to the official release, fans can get a sneak peek at the Switch version during events like Osaka Game Dungeon and Tokyo eSports Festa 2026. Will Fruit Mountain Party become the next must-have title for your gaming collection? Only time will tell!
