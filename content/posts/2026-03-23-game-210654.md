Recent rumors have surfaced regarding Sony's Firesprite Studios, revealing that the studio has reportedly cancelled two intriguing projects: a VR experience based on the iconic series Breaking Bad and an original sci-fi horror title. These cancellations come amidst significant layoffs within the PlayStation division, raising concerns about the studio's future and the impact on its creative output.

Firesprite, known for its work on Horizon: Call of the Mountain, was expected to deliver a unique VR experience that would likely have been exclusive to PSVR 2. The second cancelled project, an original sci-fi horror game, hints at the studio's ambition to explore new narratives and gameplay mechanics, although it remains unclear if this was connected to their earlier horror title, The Persistence. 

As the gaming community reflects on these developments, one can't help but wonder what the future holds for Firesprite and its potential projects. Will they bounce back from these setbacks, or is this the beginning of a more significant shift within the PlayStation landscape?
