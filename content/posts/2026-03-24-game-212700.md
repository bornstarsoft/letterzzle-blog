Cursed Words, the upcoming roguelike puzzle game from Forlife Interactive and Buried Things, is set to release on April 2, 2026, and it promises to shake up the word game genre. Drawing inspiration from the popular poker-like deckbuilder Belatro, this game combines the thrill of creating words with a unique twist, using letter tiles in a Boggle-like grid to form combinations that go beyond traditional gameplay.

What makes Cursed Words particularly intriguing is its blend of humor and creativity. Players can expect hilariously unconventional rules and abilities, including the use of numbers and chess pieces to craft bizarre words. With 11 different characters, each boasting unique powers and synergies, the game offers a plethora of combinations and experiences, ensuring that no two runs are alike. Plus, there's a free demo available on Steam, allowing players to dive into the madness before the official launch.

As we await the release, one can't help but wonder: will Cursed Words redefine how we think about word games, or will it simply be another quirky addition to the genre?
