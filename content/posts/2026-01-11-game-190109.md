The recent face-off between Triangle Math & Science Academy and Lee Early College highlights the importance of fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills among students. These competitions not only challenge students academically but also prepare them for future careers in tech and science.

Events like the Brain Game serve as a platform for students to showcase their knowledge and skills, while also encouraging teamwork and collaboration.
